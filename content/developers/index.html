Contributing packages

Bioconductor is an open development project, meaning that all developers from the scientific community are able to contribute software. Packages contributed must meet Bioconductor guidelines and undergo a peer review process. Once accepted maintainers commit to continued maintenance and involvement on the community support pages regarding their contributions.
